The Story

One Person. An Entire Department.

Domo's marketing organization is 80+ people, spanning demand gen, product marketing, content, competitive intel, paid media, and sales enablement. One person on that team built and deployed five Gemini Enterprise agents that now serve the entire department. These aren't experiments or prototypes. They're production tools the team uses every day to draft content, answer data questions, coach sales reps, and track competitors. Each agent is grounded in a Knowledge Graph so every output stays accurate and on-brand.

Gemini Enterprise Agents

Five Agents. Five Specializations.

Each agent is deployed to Gemini Enterprise and available to the team through Google Chat. They share the same Knowledge Graph backbone, but each one solves a distinct marketing challenge.

Knowledge Graph Assistant

The content engine. Drafts emails, pitch decks, battle cards, and blog posts, then automatically fact-checks every Domo claim against the Knowledge Graph before delivering. Reads uploaded PDFs, images, and URLs natively.

Live in Production

CMO Marketing Intelligence

The executive analyst. Answers any marketing performance question (attribution, pipeline, conversion, ROI) with live data and a direct link to the exact Domo dashboard card. Tested across 110 real-world questions with 100% accuracy.

Live in Production

Clanker 5000

The paid media specialist. Queries live keyword performance data across four global regions, generates Google Ads, LinkedIn ads, and display copy, and clusters thousands of keywords using semantic analysis to find optimization opportunities.

Live in Production

ADM Discovery Agent

The sales coach. Generates tailored discovery questions for Domo's field sales teams based on the prospect's industry, persona, and deal stage. All grounded in official messaging so reps always lead with the right value proposition.

Live in Production

Competitive Intel Agent

The always-on analyst. Tracks 18+ competitors using live Google Search grounding, generates battle cards on demand, monitors M&A signals, and identifies common market misconceptions. Everything is validated against the Knowledge Graph before it reaches the team.

Live in Production
Automated Workflows

Runs on Its Own. Every Day.

Beyond on-demand agents, Gemini also powers automated pipelines that run on a schedule, collecting intelligence, generating reports, and delivering insights to leadership without anyone lifting a finger.

Daily Competitive Intelligence

Every morning at 7am, Gemini researches all 18+ tracked competitors, checks for product updates, monitors M&A signals, and flags new market misconceptions. Results are written to a live database and a digest email goes out to marketing leadership.

Runs daily at 7:00 AM

Weekly Executive Report

Pulls data from live Domo dashboards, the marketing calendar, and reference documents, then generates the CMO's weekly staff report automatically. The finished report is written back to SharePoint and the team is notified.

Runs weekly on Fridays
